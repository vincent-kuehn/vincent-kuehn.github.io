I am a Master’s student in Economics at the University of Mannheim. This summer, I will begin my PhD in Economics in the Department of Economics at Washington University in St. Louis.

I am broadly interested in questions from public economics and in using modern causal inference methods to evaluate the effectiveness of public policies in environments where frictions matter.
