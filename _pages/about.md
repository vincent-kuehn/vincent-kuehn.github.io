I am a Master’s student in Economics at the University of Mannheim. This summer, I will begin my PhD in Economics in the Department of Agricultural, Environmental, and Development Economics at The Ohio State University in Columbus, Ohio.

My research interests center on how public policies affect outcomes in the environment, health, and education—and what political dynamics follow from those effects. I want to understand and apply methods that credibly evaluate policies and their causal impacts. In doing so, I hope to contribute to evidence that helps make public policy more effective and delivers the strongest possible impact for each dollar spent.
