I am a Master’s student in Economics at the University of Mannheim. This summer, I will begin my PhD in Economics in the Department of Economics at Washington University in St. Louis.

I am interested in how public policies affect environmental and health outcomes, and in using methods that credibly evaluate those policies’ causal effects when behavior, information, and other frictions matter.
